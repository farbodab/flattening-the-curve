Metric Explanations
1. Case Incidence: The total number of new cases a public health unit reports in the prior seven days.
Last updated {{timesObj.ontario_phu_weekly_new_cases}}
2. RT: A measure of how contagious a disease is at a certain point in time. It tells us the average number of people one sick person can infect (e.g. an Rt of 5 means that on average, each case is expected to infect 5 other people). If Rt is greater than 1, it means the outbreak is growing.
Last updated {{timesObj.canada_bettencourt_and_ribeiro_approach}}
3. Testing < 24h: The amount of time needed for a lab to process a specimen and enter the result (e.g. positive or negative) into the public health database. This should be as fast as possible to prevent backlogs and provide the most accurate view possible on the pandemic situation.
Last updated {{timesObj.ontario_testing_24_hours}}
4. Total ICU Bed Occupied: The percentage of beds in the intensive care unit that are occupied. This includes both COVID-19 patients and patients who are in the ICU for other reasons. This gives us an important sense of healthcare capacity since if there are not enough ICU beds, patients are at a higher risk of death.
Last updated {{timesObj.ontario_phu_icu_capacity}}

Data Sources
1. Case Incidence: Calculated using case reported date. Data from Confirmed positive cases of COVID19 in Ontario from Government of Ontario
2. Rt: Calculated using a modified approach of Kevin Systrom (based on Bettencourt & Ribeiro). Data from the COVID-19 Canada Open Data Working Group
3. Testing: Calculated using case reported date, specimen reported date, test reported date. Data from confirmed positive cases of COVID19 in Ontario from Government of Ontario
4. ICU:Calculated using number of critical care beds and critical care patients in baseline and incremental type 2 and type 3 icus. Data from Critical Care Services Ontario (CCSO)
A Note On Missing Data
DNE: Does Not Exist
A number of key data items in the scorecard above, such as contact tracing capacity, are listed as DNE, which stands for Does Not Exist. Data for these crucial indicators is currently not available or not reported publicly.
