The Pandemic is Shifting Course

Over the past few weeks, the tide of the pandemic has shifted into two distinct streams: the community at-large and congregate settings such as long-term care homes (LTCs), hospitals, homeless shelters, and prisons. People in these congregate settings are more vulnerable to COVID-19.


This graph tracks where new cases come from daily, the community at-large or LTCs and hospitals. There have been several days where the bulk of new cases are being reported from congregate settings.


It shows us that #FlatteningTheCurve must involve protecting our most vulnerable through comprehensive testing, intensive infection control, and other policies to minimize risk of transmission, such as restricting staff to one home and moving the homeless into temporary housing.
