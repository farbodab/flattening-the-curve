Which Curve Will Ontario Follow?

Once critical care (ICU) beds run out, mortality jumps from 1% to 5%.

This graph tracks ICU bed utilization in Ontario relative to the best case and worst case predictions released by the province.

The green curve is where we stand today. We can either follow the red curve or the blue curve. Decisions today will have a 1-2 week lag.

The time to #FlattenTheCurve is now.
