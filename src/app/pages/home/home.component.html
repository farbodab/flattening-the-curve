Which Curve Will Ontario Follow?

Once critical care (ICU) beds run out, mortality rate jumps from 1% to 5%.

This graph tracks ICU bed utilization in Ontario relative to the best case and worst case predictions released by the province.

The stacked bars show Ontario's confirmed and suspected cases. We can either follow the red curve or the blue curve. Decisions today will have a 1-2 week lag.

The time to #FlattenTheCurve is now.

Sources:
Confirmed ICU cases: Ontario Government
Suspected ICU cases: CCSO
Projected ICU cases: Ministry of Health Announcement - April 3, 2020
