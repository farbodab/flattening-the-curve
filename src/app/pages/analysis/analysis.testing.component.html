Testing Analysis: Ontario

A casual glance at positive tests in Ontario suggests the curve is relatively flat. Doubling time (not shown) has been decreasing. But a deeper analysis shows that delays in test results are piling up, producing an artificial decrease in the number of confirmed cases. Approximately 25% of cases are unreported.

Additionally, Ontario is not testing all patients with flu like symptoms . Even with rigorous testing, we know from Wuhan that there are approximately 10 symptomatic cases for each positive test. Ontarioâ€™s ratio is likely even higher.
