Capacity Analysis: Ontario

Researchers from the COVID-19 ModCollab have developed a compartment model to predict the time Ontario ICU resources will be depleted under undesirable (Italy) and better scenarios. They explore scenarios varying the rate of growth of patients in the community/emergency department and ICU surge capacity. The effects of social distancing are modeled by decreasing the growth rate after 14 days. Take a look below
