test_data_canada

Source: Government of Canada and News Media

Patient level data of covid cases in canada. Compiled from publicly available information on confirmed and presumptive postive cases during the ongoing COVID-19 outbreak in Canada. Data are entered with each line representing a unique case, including age, sex, health region location, and history of travel where available.
