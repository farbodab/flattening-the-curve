If we miss insights, we risk lives.

#HowsMyFlattening is an open collaboration initiative of data-savvy academia, industry and healthcare experts building a virtual war room of Ontario-relevant Covid-19 information. We are right now reactive by necessity. Ontario is producing critical data that is surging through fragmented channels with an inconsistency that sparks gaps and lags in decision making. Our crowd-sourced team of physicians, data scientists, engineers and designers are working hard to integrate information in an open-source way and are rapidly sharing in an easy-to-digest manner that speed up critical healthcare decision-making.
